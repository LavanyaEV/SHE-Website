ABOUT

SHE is a platform to promote opportunity to develop technical capabilitied and talents among female students. also to develop student leaders who will be the torch bearers for the second phase and to initiate a move for women empowerment in GCE Kannur. it encourages women enginers to take up leadership roles and promote greater enrolment of girls in engineering.

ACTIVITIES

Govt College of engineering Kannur has ahistory of almost thirty years. Firstly, it was a creation of an online network of alumnae, under the name " Women Engineers Connect". Secondly, a 24-hour Women hackathon was conducted in collaboration with ICFOSS, which also involved building assistive chatbots. Then, a "SHE-Coders" platform was initiated shortly thereafter, which provided online platform for students to develop their coding skills.
